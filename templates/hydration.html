What is Hydration Calculator?

  • A hydration calculator helps you determine your daily water needs based on factors like age, weight, activity level, and climate.

  • It provides personalized recommendations to ensure you stay adequately hydrated

  • By inputting your details, the calculator estimates how much water you should drink each day to support optimal health and prevent dehydration.

    • Risks of Inadequate Hydration

    • Dehydration:Symptoms include dry mouth, fatigue, dizziness, and dark-colored urine. Severe dehydration can lead to more serious health issues like kidney stones or heat-related illnesses.

    • Impaired Physical Performance:Dehydration can reduce endurance, strength, and coordination, making physical activities more challenging and increasing the risk of injury.

    • Cognitive Effects:Inadequate hydration can affect concentration, mood, and overall mental performance, leading to difficulties in focus and decision-making.
