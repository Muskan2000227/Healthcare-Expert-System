What is Ideal Body Weight Calculator ?

  • Ideal Body Weight refers to a weight range considered healthy for an individual based on factors like height, age, sex, and body composition.

  • It's important because it helps in assessing health risks associated with underweight or overweight conditions.

  • Maintaining an ideal body weight involves a balance of proper diet, exercise, and overall lifestyle choices.

    • Health risks related to being underweight?

    • May lead to nutritional deficiencies

    • Weakened immune system

    • Fatigue

    • Increased risk of osteoporosis

    Health risks related to being overweight?

    • Associated with a higher risk of cardiovascular diseases

    • Diabetes

    • High blood pressure

    • Joint problems
