What is Diabetes Calculator ?

  • A Diabetes Calculator is a tool used to estimate the risk of developing diabetes or to help manage diabetes.It typically includes the following features:

    • Risk Assessment

    • Blood Glucose Levels

    Health risks related to being Diabetes ?

    • Cardiovascular Diseases:Increased risk of heart attacks, strokes, and other cardiovascular conditions due to high blood sugar levels damaging blood vessels.

    • Kidney Damage:Potential for diabetic nephropathy, which can lead to kidney failure and may require dialysis.

    • Nerve Damage:Risk of peripheral neuropathy, which can cause pain, tingling, or numbness in the extremities, and can lead to infections or ulcers.

    • Vision Problems:Risk of diabetic retinopathy, which can lead to vision loss or blindness due to damage to the blood vessels in the eyes.

    • Increased Infection Risk:Higher susceptibility to infections, particularly in the skin and urinary tract, due to a weakened immune system and poor wound healing.
